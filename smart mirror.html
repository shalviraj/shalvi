Smart Mirror

Tools Used: Adobe XD, Unsplash, Mural, Photoshop

A system that enhances the home workout experience by means of an augmented mirror that features a digital touch screen interface and can detect gestures. The design intervention behind the prototype seeks to provide an efficient user-friendly gym experience at home. Like many facets of life, a gym is unfortunately made inaccessible nationally under the current Covid-19 restrictions. This design intervention intends to address and compensate for that.

Exploration & Design

Moving ahead with the idea of smart mirror, we had another brainstorming session around the concept. It helped us develop the idea and complicate our understanding of it which revealed greater complexity and a wealth of direction (Annexure 1). We then returned to Microsoft teams with some good ideas that we felt strongly about and some more absurd ideas to evoke discussion and thought and possibly discover new ground.
